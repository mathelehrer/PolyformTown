Promote vcomp toward core after index behavior stabilizes

Vertex completion should become core functionality once RL0 index
construction works. The time to move it is after index behavior is
validated, because indexes change how possible neighbors on a frontier
or boundary are enumerated.

This promotion will likely require generalizing vcomp and some boundary
utilities together. Keep vcomp available to the vcomp pipeline, print,
and count tools during the transition, but move stable mathematical
functionality toward core.
